A Letter For You 💌

03/04/2026

Mere Kinni,

Mujhe nahi pata mein kahan se shuru karun... par jo bhi likh rahi hoon, wo dil ki sachchai hai.

Tumse milkar hi maine jaana ki prem kya hota hai.
na dharm, na jaati, na koi seema,
prem bas hota hai... aur jab hota hai, to poori tarah hota hai.

Tum wo wajah ho, jisse maine ye sab mehsoos kiya,
aur shayad isi wajah se aaj bhi,
agar ye rishta dard deta hai,
to bhi main ise chhodne ke liye taiyaar nahi hoon aur kabhi taiyaar rahungi bhi nahi.

Haan, muje patha hei rastha bahoth hi mushkil hein,
aur tum isiliye muje dhuur rakhna chahte ho
par tumhare saath muje har mushkil bhi manzoor hei
kyunki muje pata hei ki agar tum mere sath ho,
tho mein kisi ka bhi samna kar sakthi huun

Haan, tumse shikayat bhi hai,
par usse kahin zyada tumse mohabbat hai.
Agar tumse koi galti ho jaaye,
to bhi mera dil tumhe chhodna nahi chahta,
balki tumhare saath hi aage badhna chahta hai.

Tum har pal mere khayalon mein rehte ho,
subah ki shuruaat se lekar raat ke aakhri khayal tak.

haan,Tum abhi mujse baath nahi kar rahe ho,
ignore kar rahe ho muje, ye sochke ki mein ek din tumhe bhool jaungi aur move on karungi
par shayad tumhe patha nahi hei pyar ka shuruaath rehthi hein par anth nahi

Tum mere liye waise hi ho
jaise zindagi ke liye hawa.

Mujhe pata hai ki tum mujhse bahut pyaar karte ho,
par tumhara parivaar tumhare aur mere beech khada hai.
Tum mujhe us sangharsh mein nahi daalna chahte,
par kya bina lade haar maan lena sahi hai?

Kehte hain,
jo dil se chaho, uske liye ladna padta hai,
kuch bhi aasan nahi hota,
aur pyaar to bilkul bhi nahi.

Mujhe pata hai ye raah mushkil hai,
par kya ye naamumkin hai?
Phir tum mujhe beech raaste mein yun kaise chhod sakte ho?

Main bhi chahti hoon ki sab saath hon,
hamara rishta kisi ke khilaaf nahi,
sabke saath poora ho,
par kya bina koshish kiye hi peeche hat jaana sahi hai?

Ab kuch sawaal hain tumse..

Jis ladki ke saath tumhe sukoon mila,
jiske saath tum khud ban paaye,
jise tumne apna ghar jaisa mehsoos kiya,
use tum yun kaise chhod sakte ho?

Jise maine apni poori duniya maana,
jiske saath har sapna dekha,
jiske uuper bharosa rakke,apne aap ko samarpith kiya
kya samaaj ki khushi ke liye
tum use yun hi jaane doge?

Tumne kaha tha,
har haal mein saath rahoge,
har mushkil se ladoge,
to ab ye peeche hatna kyon?

Un hansi ke palon ka kya,
un yaadon ka kya,
us ehsaas ka kya jo humne saath jiya,
kya "move on" sach mein itna aasan hota hai?

Main aaj bhi wahin khadi hoon,
jahan tumne mujhe chhod diya tha.

Ant mein bas itna,
mera pyaar aaj bhi wahin hai,
tumhare saath, tumhare liye.

Ab faisla tumhara hai,
par jaane se pehle
mere in sawaalon ka jawab zaroor dena.

Bhavdiya,
Tumhari Ammu
